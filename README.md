Neon Velocity Typer is a high-octane, browser-based typing game designed to test your speed (WPM) and accuracy. Built with a "Supercode" philosophy, it packs advanced features like audio synthesis, music visualization, and particle physics into a single, lightweight HTML file.

Created by rcwpt.

üéÆ Gameplay

Dual Language Support: Seamlessly switch between Indonesian (ID) and English (EN) content.

Adaptive Difficulty: The game evolves with you. As your score increases, words become longer and more complex.

Combo System: Maintain your streak to increase your score multiplier (up to 5x).

Performance Analysis: Get detailed feedback on your WPM (Words Per Minute) and Accuracy with tiered rankings (from "Basic" to "God Tier").

üéµ Audio & Visuals

Synthesized SFX: No external audio files required! All sound effects (clicks, success, errors) are generated in real-time using the Web Audio API.

Music Player Integration:

Local File: Play your own .mp3 or .wav files.

YouTube: Input a YouTube ID to stream background music.

Real-time Visualizer: A dynamic frequency bar visualizer that reacts to your local music.

Juicy Effects: Particle explosions, screen shake, and neon glow effects powered by Tailwind CSS.
